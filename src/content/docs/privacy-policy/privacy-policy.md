# Privacy Policy for Summarizerrrr

**Last Updated: December 19, 2025**

This privacy policy explains how Summarizerrrr ("the Extension") collects, uses, and protects your information.

## Information Collection and Use

The Extension does **not** collect any personal identifying information from its users. All data processed by the Extension, such as video transcripts or web page content, is processed locally within your browser.

### Data Sent to Third-Party AI Services

When you use the summarization feature, the content you choose to summarize (such as video transcripts, article text, or web page content) is sent to the AI provider you have configured. The Extension supports multiple AI providers:

- **Google Gemini** - Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **OpenAI (ChatGPT)** - Subject to [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy/)
- **Anthropic (Claude)** - Subject to [Anthropic's Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)
- **Groq** - Subject to [Groq's Terms of Service](https://groq.com/terms-of-use/)
- **Cerebras** - Subject to [Cerebras' Terms](https://cerebras.ai/terms)
- **DeepSeek** - Subject to [DeepSeek's Privacy Policy](https://www.deepseek.com/privacy)
- **OpenRouter** - Subject to [OpenRouter's Privacy Policy](https://openrouter.ai/privacy)
- **Ollama / LM Studio** - Runs entirely locally on your device, no data is sent externally

**Important:** The Extension only sends content to AI providers when you explicitly request a summary. No data is sent automatically or in the background.

## Data Storage

The Extension stores the following data **locally** in your browser using Chrome's storage API:

- **User Preferences:** Theme, language, display settings
- **API Keys:** Your personal API keys (stored securely in local storage, never transmitted to our servers)
- **Summarization History (Archive):** If enabled, your past summaries are stored locally
- **Custom Prompts:** Any custom prompts you create

This data remains on your device and is **never** transmitted to external servers operated by us.

## Permissions Explained

The Extension requests the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `activeTab` | To read content from the current tab for summarization |
| `storage` | To save your preferences and API keys locally |
| `scripting` | To extract content from web pages |
| `tabs` | To detect URL changes for YouTube navigation |
| `sidePanel` | To display the summarization interface |
| `contextMenus` | To add right-click menu options |
| `host_permissions` | To communicate with AI provider APIs |

## No Tracking or Analytics

The Extension does **not**:
- Collect usage analytics
- Track your browsing history
- Use cookies for tracking
- Share data with advertisers
- Require account registration

## Data Security

- API keys are stored locally and encrypted
- All communication with AI providers uses HTTPS
- No data is stored on external servers operated by us

## Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this page periodically.
